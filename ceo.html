Connect to your Google Sheet

This dashboard reads directly from your BEL Daily Diary Google Sheet. You need to publish the sheet as a CSV so this page can read it. Takes 2 minutes — one time only.

How to get your Sheet URL:
1. Open your BEL Daily Diary Google Sheet
2. Click File → Share → Publish to web
3. Set the first dropdown to Sheet1, second to Comma-separated values (.csv)
4. Click Publish → copy the URL it gives you
5. Paste it below and click Connect
The URL looks like: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/SHEET_ID/pub?gid=0&single=true&output=csv
